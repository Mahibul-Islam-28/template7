About IDS

About IDS

At IDS Group, we are proud to be one of the leading business group in Bangladesh with the experience of about 25 years are known for our strong presence in the RMG, Real Estate and IT sectors. With years of expertise in these industries, we have earned a reputation for delivering high-quality products and services to our clients.

Our commitment to excellence has driven us to create our brand IDS Builders Ltd to take on large-scale real estate development projects, such as shopping malls and condominiums. IDS Ashulia Mall is one of our country-wide shopping mall chain. Our team of skilled professionals work tirelessly to ensure that every project meets the highest standards of quality and excellence.

Whether you are a property owner looking to develop your land or a business, seeking the perfect location for your next project, IDS Builders Ltd. is the ideal partner for you. We are dedicated to help our clients achieve their goals and we are always ready to go the extra mile to ensure their complete satisfaction.

At IDS Builders Ltd., we understand that building and development projects can be complex and challenging. That's why we work closely with our clients to understand their needs, goals and aspirations and why we provide personalized, one-on-one support throughout the entire process. Our aim is to take the stress and hassle out of your project, so that you can focus on what really matters - your business and your life.

We believe that the key to our success is our passion for what we do and our commitment to make a positive impact in the communities we serve. Whether it's through the creation of new jobs, the improvement of local infrastructure or simply by building better places for people to live, work, and play.

Our Values

IDS Builders Ltd our values define who we are and guide everything we do. They serve as a foundation for our business practices and help us achieve our mission, vision and goals. Our values include: excellence: we strive for excellence in all that we do and are committed to delivering the highest quality products and services to our clients.
Integrity: we are honest and transparent in all of our business dealings and always act with the highest ethical standards.
Innovation: we are a forward-thinking company that embraces new ideas and approaches and continuously seeks ways to improve our processes and products. Collaboration: we believe that great things can be achieved through teamwork and are committed to fostering a culture of collaboration and mutual respect.
Service: we are dedicated to serve our clients and to provide them with the best possible customer experience.
Sustainability: we develop all of our real estate project keeping priority on practice of incorporating environmentally-friendly design techniques, materials and technologies into the building process.
These values drive everything we do at IDS Builders Ltd and help us to achieve our mission and vision. They are a testament to our commitment to excellence and to our dedication to making a positive impact in the world.

The journey as IDS Group has been one of growth, innovation and commitment to excellence. Founded in the heart of Dhaka city, we started out in the RMG Sector with a passion for creating a difference on quality, commitment and values. As we expanded our operations, we diversified into new industries including Real Estate and IT Solutions. This allowed us to bring IDS Builders Ltd for utilizing our wider range of expertise and experience for large-scale real estate development work and to better serve our clients. Over the years, we have tackled complex projects and have consistently delivered outstanding results. Our commitment to innovation, excellence and ethical business practices has helped us to establish a strong reputation in the industry and to become a trusted partner for businesses and communities alike. We are proud of the progress we have made thus far and we are confident in our ability to continue to grow and evolve. At IDS Builders Ltd, our journey is not just about creating great real estate developments, but about making a positive impact in the world and helping to build a brighter future for all.
