IDS Ashulia Mall

Mission of IDS Ashulia Mall Project

To make Ashulia self-sufficient and reduce load of Dhaka City in order to enhance Ashulia's inhabitants living standard by creating entrepreneurship and employment.

Vision of IDS Ashulia Mal.

To be the leading eco-friendly popular mall by satisfying our buyers and customers with unique shopping, leisure, health-wellness experience offering quality, variety, price, service & safety.

Project Consultants

Design Source (Architect), Engr. Saiful Bari (Structural) & BN Engineering (MEP) Engr. Zoha (Electrical), IECL (Fire Detection & Protection System).

Location

Noroshingopur, Dhaka-Ashulia Highway, Ashulia, Savar, Dhaka.

Ashulia Destination for the future

  • Ashulia is the future commercial, entertainment & technology hub of the country.
  • After completion of metro project, it is just a 30 minutes journey from Motijheel.
  • Will be connected with Gazipur to Motijheel via Abdullahpur by express elevate-way connectivity
  • Sadarghat-Turag water taxi project is about to be started soon
  • Just 10 km driving distance from Shajalalal International Airport
  • As industrial hub, Ashulia is an area of high income class and high literacy rate
  • Centre of the city will be shifted to Ashulia and surrounding areas within next 10 years
